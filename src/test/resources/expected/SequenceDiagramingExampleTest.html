Bambam gets food for his dad

Note that this test may make you feel hungry
For related test class see Specification example test

Specification

Given mr flintstone is "hungry"
When he harasses bambam for food
Then bambam places a burger order with barney "a burger"

When barney gives to bambam "1 burger"
Then bambam gives to mr flintstone "1 burger"
Then mr flintstone shares his food with barney because he likes him

Test result:

Test passed
